THE CONSTITUTION

ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

UPV Komsai.Org

UPV Komsai.Org is a non-profit organization for B.S. in Computer Science students, faculty, staff, and alumni of University of the Philippines Visayas. UPV Komsai.Org has always aimed to provide an avenue for academic excellence, social interaction, and future career resilience. The organization remains consistent in spearheading activities regarding important trends and topics in the fields of Information Technology and Computer Science, and we have had partnered with companies and experts in the tech industry.

After a year since it was founded during the first academic year of 2000, UPV Komsai.Org has extended its membership to include not only the UPV student population but also to other schools and private individuals who also share its cause to provide a support group to students and individuals related to IT. The basic principle is that if an individual shares his or her knowledge to another person, they both acquire new knowledge and another new knowledge is formed from the fusion of the two ideas. It would be only logical that if the same concept, if applied to a group of individuals, a massive amount of new ideas would be generated, uplifting the level of consciousness of each individual in the group. Making ourselves better individuals, better persons to others and making a bit of difference to life.
